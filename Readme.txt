Sounds files sourced from https://www.zapsplat.com
Non-provided sprites sourced from:  https://opengameart.org/users/bonsaiheldin

A score of 100 wins the game.

WASD to move on keyboard
Q to fire on keyboard

Left thumbstick to move on a gamepad
A Button to fire on a gamepad.

Player can survive colliding with two enemies before death.

Futures plans are to add vector movement and interpolation, more enemies, difficulty levels
more levels and general optimisation tweaks
